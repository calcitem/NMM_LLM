## Session
- Winner and result pattern: Black wins in 6 moves.
- Opening or early-game pattern: White plays a4 and Black defends with d2.
- One key turning point: White's move on d7 creates a potential mill.
- One lesson about mobility, initiative, or mill timing: Mill requires the ability to quickly move 3 pieces in a row and then remove one opponent piece.
- One note about the human's habits or improvements: The human tends to place their pieces in the center and then focus on expanding their lines. This can make it difficult to create a mill and remove a piece from the center.
